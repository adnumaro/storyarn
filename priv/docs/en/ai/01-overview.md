Storyarn's AI features are being rolled out gradually. This section documents them as they become available.

## AI provider integrations

Connect your own AI provider accounts (API keys) in **Account settings → AI Integrations**. Keys are encrypted at rest, validated before saving, and revocable at any time.

## AI actions

AI actions appear as commands in the command palette as they ship. Each action states what it does and what it costs before it runs.
