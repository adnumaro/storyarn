Storyarn is a **narrative design platform** for game designers and interactive storytellers. It brings all your narrative work into one place — characters, dialogue, world maps, scripts, and translations.

Whether you're building an RPG, a visual novel, or an adventure game, Storyarn gives you the tools to design your story visually and collaboratively.

---

## The big picture

Most teams piece together their narrative across spreadsheets, flowchart apps, Google Docs, and custom tools. Storyarn replaces all of that with a unified platform where everything stays connected.

Define a character's stats in a **Sheet**. Reference those stats in a **Flow** to create branching dialogue. Place that location on a **Scene** map. Export the script as a **Screenplay**. Translate it all with the **Localization** tools.

Change a character's health value once, and every flow that checks it reflects the update immediately.

---

## Core tools

**Sheets** hold your structured data — character profiles, item databases, location details. Every field becomes a variable that other tools can read and modify.

**Flows** are visual dialogue trees. You connect nodes to build branching conversations, add conditions to react to game state, and use instructions to change it.

**Scenes** are spatial canvases for your world. Place zones, mark points of interest, and define how players navigate between locations.

**Screenplays** let you write in industry-standard screenplay format, with Fountain export for professional tools.

**Localization** extracts all your texts, translates them with DeepL integration, and tracks progress across languages.

---

## Who is it for?

Storyarn works for anyone involved in interactive storytelling — narrative designers, game writers, world builders, localization teams, and small studios that want one tool instead of five.
