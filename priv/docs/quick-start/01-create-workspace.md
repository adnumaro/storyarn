A workspace is your team's home base. It holds your projects and controls who has access.

---

## Create your workspace

After signing in, click **New Workspace** and give it a name. A URL slug is generated automatically.

That's it — you have a workspace.

---

## Create a project

From the workspace dashboard, click **New Project** and enter a name.

Each project is fully isolated with its own sheets, flows, scenes, and screenplays. One workspace can hold as many projects as you need.

---

## Invite your team

Go to **Settings → Members** and click **Invite**. Enter an email and choose a role.

- **Owner** — full control, including billing and deletion
- **Admin** — manage members, create and delete projects
- **Member** — edit content in projects they have access to
- **Viewer** — read-only access everywhere

Invitations expire after 7 days and can be revoked anytime.

---

## Project-level access

Within each project, you can further control permissions. A workspace Member can be an Editor on one project but a Viewer on another.

- **Owner** — full project control
- **Editor** — create and edit content
- **Viewer** — read-only
